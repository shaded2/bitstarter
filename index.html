Introducing Tasquid - Staging Version

The cure for your Astrid blues

So what is Tasquid?

Tasquid is a the reincarnation of the most beloved TODO list application that ever was! Of course we're talking about Astrid. Some time around May 2013 Yahoo purchased Astrid and soon announced that the company's products will be terminated. Over 4 million users of the product were left to scour their respective App stores for the myriad of alternatives that lived in Astrid's shadow. Astrid fans have taken to the company's facebook page to accounce their dis-pleasure and to plead their case to Yahoo. A number of Astrid users have also stated that there are currently no suitable replacements to the Astrid product.

Why Tasquid?

The folks behind Tasquid loved the Astrid product dearly and did not want to see it die. So they did what any passionate group of developers would do. They Forked it! The rebranded App is now available for free in the Android Market. The team is hard at work on a solution to sync with an opensource Web based application. The mid term goal is to restore, most of the products features as soon as posible.

FAQ

What is Astrid?
As millions of users have discovered, Astrid is more than just the perfect to-do list -- it's the personal assistant you've always wanted. People use it for groceries, errands, and all their lists - to read, to watch, to visit, to-do! Astrid helps you get your life organized so you will never miss a deadline with fun reminders, subtasks, lists, and widgets. Then access your tasks anywhere with automatic syncing across your phone, tablet, and Astrid.com. We can also all use a little help to be extra productive -- use Astrid to collaborate on projects with your friends and loved ones. Divide up tasks for that trip you're trying to plan with your best friends… Astrid reminds them so you don't have to. And when your sweetheart picks up the dry cleaning, Astrid notifies you!
What exactly are you selling?
We are not selling anything. We loved astrid and figure thousands if not millions of others did too. Therefore we are asking for help to start a movement that will hopefully product alive and kiking forever. We will provide for a fee an optional hosted solution that will keep your tasks in sync across devices and the web.
Will this be just like astrid?
The Android app will be exactly like Astrid, except for a different logo. The Website will be powered by Tracks Software instead of Astrid.com
How do we know you will not sell this to the next highest bidder?
There is nothing to sell. The software (both Android and Web) will be completely opensource for anywone to use, contribute to and improve.
Will this be available for ios?
Unfortunately Astrid did not opensource the IOS software. So Taskquid will not be available for IOS right not. This may change in the future.
